Tex-Mex Goulash

Prep Time 10 mins.
Cook Time 25 mins.
Serves 6

This variation on a classic Americanized Hungarian recipe is perfect for anyone who loves spice, non-traditional Mexican dishes, and pasta. As colorful as it is hip, this dish is a splendid escape from the cold, dark recesses of winter. Did we mention this recipe is gluten free?

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. hamburger
  • 1 lb. corn macaroni
  • 1 medium red onion, chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 poblano pepper, chopped
  • 1 large tomato, seeds removed, chopped
  • 1 can (4 oz.) diced jalapenos, drained
  • 1 can spicy Mexican tomato sauce
  • 2-3 tbsp. taco seasoning

Try this instead!

Substitute the burger with black beans for a high-fiber, gluten free vegan adventure!

Directions

  1. In a large pot, brown the hamburger. When the burger is almost brown, add the onion.
  2. In another large pot, bring 4 quarts of water to a boil.
  3. While the water is coming to a boil, add the taco seasoning and dice jalapenos to the browned hamburger. Reduce heat to a simmer.
  4. When the water begins to boil, add the macaroni and boil for 9 minutes.
  5. While the macaroni is boiling, add the red bell pepper, poblano pepper, and tomato sauce. Continue to simmer.
  6. When the macaroni is done boiling, strain it and add it to the hamburger mixture.
  7. Add the chopped tomato and simmer for another minute.
  8. Serve in a bowl with cheese and sour cream.
